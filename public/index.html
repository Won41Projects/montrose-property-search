    About these estimates

    Simplified, approximate curves for community education. Individual outcomes vary by age, severity, comorbidities, and local EMS/hospital performance.

    Anchor table (15, 30, 60, 120, 180 min)

    Minutes Stroke STEMI survival Serious injury Cardiac arrest
    1570%95%90%50–60%
    3060%90%85%40–50%

    12045%70%65%5–10%
    18040%60%55%1–3%

    Primary sources

    • HERMES pooled trials / SWIFT-EVT — stroke thrombectomy time-to-outcome
    • NCDR, NEJM, Lancet — STEMI door-to-balloon / ischemic time
    • Trauma center and prehospital care literature — time to definitive trauma care
    • AHA / CARES — cardiac arrest survival and CPR/AED timing

    Limits

    • All percentages are approximate population estimates.
    • Cardiac arrest assumes delayed CPR/AED; immediate bystander action greatly improves survival.
    • Call EMS — do not delay for self-transport.
